History

Located at the Rojhan, District Rajanpur, Punjab in Pakistan, Center of Excellence School, a project of Daanish school system established in july 2015, is a place to focus, solidify and add quality, variety, and scope to students' lives.The teachers and administration is committed to help each individual student to prove the best.There are two separate sections, senior section (4-10) with male teaching staff and junior section (Nursery - 3) with female teaching staff.
